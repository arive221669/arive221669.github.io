Damien's Fire Breakfast Restaurant has been around for over 20 hardworking years, cooking and treating our customers with kindness and respect. Damien's Fire takes pride in the way that it serves its customers and with the food that it provides for the community.

Damien's Fire Breakfast Restaurant is a child friendly place. We enjoy having anyone that would like to dine within our establishment. Please come by and visit, sit down and enjoy our breakfast selections. We have selections from eggs to different kinds of pancakes to different assortments of sandwiches. If you're not hungry we have selections of beverages for you to enjoy as well.
